# 📖 Iqraya: The AI Ustadz for Precision Quranic Recitation

**Iqraya** is a modern Quran platform designed to guide and support your every recitation with the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

---

## 🎯 Background (The Problem)

Many Muslims want to improve their Quranic recitation but are often unsure where to start. Self-studying at home frequently breeds uncertainty, as there is no ustadz or teacher available to correct pronunciation directly.

## 💡 Solution (The Solution)

Iqraya is here to take the anxiety out of learning Quranic recitation. Acting as a personal **"AI Ustadz"** available 24/7, Iqraya listens to your voice and provides instant pronunciation corrections and feedback. Currently, Iqraya is available as a flexible and practical **Web** version for daily use.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🎙️ **AI Ustadz (Interactive AI Assistant):** Simply click the microphone button and recite a verse. The AI will listen, process your voice, and provide real-time corrections.
- 📱 **Multi-Platform Access (Web & Android):** Full flexibility for your convenience. Get instant access via a _browser_ on any device without any installation required.
- 🖥️ **Clean & Focused UI/UX:** A clean, modern, and eye-friendly digital interface. Intuitive navigation is meticulously designed to keep your daily recitation experience deeply focused and free of distractions.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

### 🌐 Via Web Browser

1. Visit the Iqraya website using the browser on your PC, tablet, or smartphone.
2. Find and click the **Microphone Button**.
3. Recite the holy Quranic verse and receive instant feedback from the AI.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend Web:** React / Next.js / Tailwind CSS
- **AI Engine:** Google Gemini API
- **Deployment & Hosting:** Google Cloud Run
